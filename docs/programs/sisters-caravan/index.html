Sisters Caravan

Draft — pending approval
ScheduleMonthly
AudienceWomen of all ages and backgrounds
RegistrationVia events calendar and social media

Sisters Caravan is a monthly gathering created to nurture faith, companionship, and spiritual growth among women. Rooted in the Islamic tradition and centered on meaningful connection, the program offers a welcoming space for women to learn, reflect, and journey together in faith.

Through conversations, study, remembrance, and shared experiences, Sisters Caravan encourages women to deepen their relationship with Allah while building sincere relationships with one another. Gatherings provide opportunities for spiritual renewal, personal reflection, and cultivating a sense of belonging within the community.

What to expect

Each gathering offers a unique opportunity for learning, reflection, and connection. Programs may include discussions, guest speakers, spiritual reminders, devotional gatherings, and opportunities for meaningful conversation and companionship.

Example topics

Spiritual growth and worship · the lives of righteous women · family and relationships · personal reflection and self-development · faith in contemporary life · women's health and well-being · motherhood and caregiving · community and sisterhood · Prophetic character and spiritual refinement.

Special gatherings

Throughout the year, Sisters Caravan hosts special events and collaborative programs, including gatherings during Rabi al-Awwal, Ramadan programs, visiting scholars, devotional celebrations, and partnerships with organizations dedicated to women's Islamic learning.

Information about upcoming gatherings can be found through our events calendar and social media channels.
