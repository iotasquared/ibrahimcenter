Friday Prayer & Community (Jumu'ah)

Draft — pending approval
ScheduleEvery Friday, 1:30 PM
AudienceEveryone — long-time community, first-time visitors, families and children
Registrationdrop-in

Every Friday, our community gathers for Jumu'ah, the weekly congregational prayer and sermon that brings Muslims together in worship, reflection, and fellowship. It is a time to pause from the busyness of the week, reconnect with Allah, and gather in the company of others seeking faith and spiritual renewal.

Whether you have been part of Ibrahim Center for years or are joining us for the first time, you are warmly welcomed. Each week features a sermon from one of our teachers or guest speakers, offering timeless Islamic wisdom and practical guidance for modern life.

Jumu'ah prayers are currently held in person. Please follow our social media channels for updates on guest speakers, special events, and occasional livestreamed programs.

Jumu'ah Prayer begins at 1:30 PM every Friday at Ibrahim Center, 618 Baca Street, Houston, Texas. Parking is available on-site and along surrounding neighborhood streets — please be mindful of our neighbors.

Families and children are warmly welcomed. Our Friday gatherings feature a rotating group of scholars, teachers, and community leaders who offer sermons and reflections grounded in the Islamic tradition and relevant to modern life.
