# Get Connected — routing doctrine

Purpose (workbook §11): help every visitor reach the correct person without having to understand the organization first. Collect only what routing needs: name, email/phone, preferred contact, interest, message.

The routing table above is the grants table of the future fact-graph inbox: each interest is a lane with a named owner, a response expectation, and a backup. Nothing ships until every lane has an owner — an unrouted form is a broken promise with a submit button.
