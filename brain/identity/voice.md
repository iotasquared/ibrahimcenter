# Voice

The website should feel rooted in traditional Islam, warm, hospitable, spiritually serious but accessible, beautiful, creative, family-centered, trustworthy, organized, and welcoming to newcomers and non-Muslims.

**Words to use:** rooted, welcoming, sacred learning, companionship, service, belonging.

**Avoid:** corporate language, vague spirituality, overly academic phrasing, culturally inaccessible references, insider shorthand, over-casual tone. No pressure language anywhere (especially donations and New to Islam). Tone is invitational, never policing.
