# Menaissance

Menaissance is a monthly gathering for men seeking to cultivate faith, brotherhood, and purposeful living. Rooted in the Islamic tradition, these sessions explore what it means to embody healthy masculinity, spiritual responsibility, and sincere companionship in the modern world.

Through honest conversations, practical reflection, and sacred knowledge, men are invited to examine their relationship with Allah, themselves, their families, and their communities in an environment of sincerity, accountability, and support.

## What to expect

Each gathering combines Islamic guidance, thoughtful discussion, and meaningful brotherhood. Sessions may include lectures, group conversations, reflection exercises, and opportunities to build lasting relationships with other men pursuing spiritual growth.

## Topics

Topics may include: spiritual discipline and consistency in worship · purpose, ambition, and meaningful work · marriage and relationships · fatherhood and family leadership · brotherhood and sacred companionship · technology, distraction, and digital habits · emotional and mental well-being · anxiety, stress, and burnout · navigating loneliness and isolation · masculinity and spiritual responsibility · character development and self-accountability · balancing faith, work, and modern life.

While each session is unique, Menaissance seeks to help men become more grounded in their faith, more present in their relationships, and more responsible to the people and communities they serve.

**Frequency:** Monthly. **Audience:** Open to brothers ages 18 and older. Registration information and upcoming topics can be found through our Events page and social media channels.
