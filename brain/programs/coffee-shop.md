# The Coffee Shop

Our coffee shop is open every Friday from 12:30 PM to 3:00 PM, offering a welcoming space to gather before and after Jumu'ah. Whether you are meeting a friend, introducing yourself for the first time, or simply taking a moment to slow down, we invite you to enjoy a cup of coffee and the company of the community.

At Ibrahim Center, we believe sacred companionship often begins around a shared table. Our cafe exists as a place to connect, reflect, and continue the conversations that begin in prayer.
