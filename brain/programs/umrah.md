# Umrah — A Journey to the Two Holy Cities

Join us for a sacred Umrah journey to Makkah and Madinah, led by Shaykh Khalis Rashaad. This 7-night experience offers the blessing of visiting the two most beloved cities in Islam — the noble place of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and the House of Allah ﷻ in al-Masjid al-Haram.

## Four nights in Madinah

The journey begins with four nights in Madinah, the radiant city of the Prophet ﷺ. Pilgrims will pray in Masjid An-Nabawi, spend time in remembrance, and take part in guided visits to historical and spiritually significant sites, including locations tied to the early Muslim community. This time in Madinah offers space for peace, reflection, preparation, and love for the Prophet ﷺ.

## Three nights in Makkah

We then travel to Makkah Mukarramah for three nights, where pilgrims will perform Umrah under the guidance of Shaykh Khalis Rashaad, with explanations and reminders throughout the journey. In Makkah, we will also visit sacred and historical landmarks that connect our hearts to the legacy of faith established by Prophet Ibrahim (ʿalayhi as-salām) and renewed by the Messenger ﷺ.

> "This blessed journey invites pilgrims to worship, reflect, and renew their commitment to Allah ﷻ and His Beloved ﷺ."

## Packages

Land packages per person: Quad $2,250 · Triple $2,350 · Double $2,550 · Single $3,535. Families are encouraged to join — children 0–2 free, ages 3–9 $750. US citizens can process a free visiting visa upon entry; green-card holders should plan a self-organized umrah visa (~$225). Airfare is self-organized.

Included: accommodation and guided tours in both cities, a virtual pre-departure session, daily buffet breakfast, on-site lectures and khatirahs, Haramain high-speed train or bus transfer, dinner experiences, and all ground transportation.
