# Biography

Fatima Lakhani serves as the Youth Director, lead educator, and program coordinator for Little Stewards at Ibrahim Center, helping cultivate spaces where children and families can develop a lasting love for Islam, community, and the masjid.

Her work focuses on creating engaging, developmentally appropriate learning experiences that introduce children to faith through storytelling, creativity, play, and meaningful relationships. Through puppetry, crafts, interactive activities, and hands-on learning, she helps young Muslims build connections to Allah, the Prophets, the Companions, and the beauty of God's creation.

Fatima has played an integral role in the growth and development of Little Stewards, helping establish a welcoming environment where children feel safe, valued, and excited to learn. Her work emphasizes curiosity, compassion, confidence, and a sense of belonging within the Muslim community.

She has also helped lead children's programming during family retreats and special events, creating experiences that encourage teamwork, exploration, and spiritual growth while helping children engage with the world through love, wonder, and faith.

Through her dedication to children and families, Fatima continues to help Ibrahim Center nurture the next generation of Muslims by creating spaces where learning, joy, and spiritual growth go hand in hand.
