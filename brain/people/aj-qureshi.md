# Biography

A.J. is a native Houstonian with a deep passion for people and community. Known for his warm and inviting personality and love for building meaningful connections, he strives to make Ibrahim Center a welcoming home for everyone who walks through its doors.

With a background in counseling, psychology, and human development, he finds purpose in being of service to others through guidance, encouragement, and simply being present. Whether hosting events as an emcee, helping organize programs, or walking alongside others in their personal growth, A.J approaches his work with a heart for service.

He is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling & Psychology, reflecting his ongoing commitment to supporting the emotional, spiritual, and personal growth of others.

His goal is to help create an environment where faith and friendship grow side by side, and where every member of the community feels seen, supported, and truly at home.
