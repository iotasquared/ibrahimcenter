# Audiences (workbook §1.1, status: in progress, owner Amna)

Primary audiences: people interested in Islam; neighbors who see the new building; first-time visitors; converts and new Muslims; Houston Muslim community members; people referred by others; families; and donors.

The main action we want a **first-time visitor** to take: RSVP to an event.
The main action we want a **donor** to take: support our center.

These audience doors are stabler than any program — navigation and page priorities organize around them (Visit · New to Islam · Little Stewards/families · Donate · Get Connected), so program churn never forces an IA rebuild.
