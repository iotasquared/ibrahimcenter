# Questions Are Welcome Here

Whether you are exploring Islam, have recently embraced it, or love someone who has — you are welcome at Ibrahim Center. You do not need to know anything about Islam to reach out, and no question is too small or too simple to ask.

Ibrahim Center was founded by converts. Walking with people at the very beginning of their journey is not a side program here — it is part of who we are. You will not be handed a stack of books and sent on your way. You will be met by a person.

There is no pressure and no timeline. Come with your curiosity, your doubts, and your questions, and we will meet you where you are.

## First steps

1. **Say hello.** Reach out through our [Get Connected](/get-connected/) page, or simply come by. You do not need to explain yourself or have anything figured out first.
2. **Visit on a Friday.** Join us for Jumu'ah and stay for coffee afterward. [Here is what to expect](/visit/) so nothing feels unfamiliar.
3. **Sit down with someone.** When you are ready, we will connect you with a real person — for a conversation, a class, or companionship as you learn.

## Common questions

**Do I need to know anything about Islam to visit or reach out?**
No. Not a thing. Come as you are — curiosity is enough.

**I think I want to become Muslim. What do I do?**
You do not need to have it all worked out. Reach out, and we will talk with you, answer your questions, and — if and when you are ready — support you in taking your shahada, the testimony of faith by which one enters Islam. There is no pressure and no rush.

**Can I come if I am not Muslim, or just exploring?**
Yes, warmly. Many people visit us while learning, and family members of new Muslims are always welcome. You are our guest.

**What support is there after I embrace Islam?**
A great deal — foundations classes, mentorship and companionship, an introduction to the community, and spiritual care. You will not walk it alone.
